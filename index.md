Hi! My name is Zhouyu Li(李周宇). I am a Ph.D. student at the Computer Science Department of [North Carolina State University](https://www.ncsu.edu/), advised by [Dr. Ruozhou Yu](https://people.engr.ncsu.edu/ryu5/).
Before coming to NCSU, I received my Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering at
Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by [Dr. Sauvik Das](https://sauvikdas.com/). I received my Bachelor’s degree in Electronic Information Engineering at Central South University under the supervision of [Dr. Jun Peng](https://faculty.csu.edu.cn/pengjun1/en/index.htm) and Dr. Guoming Zhi.  

### Research Interests
I have a broad range of research interests. My research majorly covers the following topics:
* **Pervasive privacy-preserving systems**: secure and usable mobile application, privacy-preserving video analytics, and privacy-preserving Internet widgets. 
* **Optimization for morden and emerging networks**: [microservice-based distributed systems](https://ncsu-microservice-benchmarking.github.io/Microservice-Profiling-with-Distributed-Tracing-and-Kubernetes/), quantum network optimization.

Generally, I am devoted to building easy-to-use, efficient, and secure communication systems that bring people closer to each other.
